# Product Context

## Vision

Notism is a food ordering platform for a single restaurant, solving the friction of ordering food for customers who want a fast, simple experience. Customers can browse the menu, place orders online, and pay via card or cash on delivery — all without needing to call or visit in person. The platform also gives restaurant staff the tools to manage and fulfil incoming orders efficiently.

## Core Value Proposition

The fastest, simplest way to order from Notism — browse, order, and pay online or cash on delivery in seconds.

## Business Model

Notism is the restaurant. Revenue is generated directly from food sales made through the platform.

## Business Goals

- Grow the volume of online orders placed through the platform
- Streamline internal operations and reduce manual order management effort
- Minimise time from order placement to delivery or pickup

## Target Users

### Primary: Consumers / End Customers

People who want to order food from Notism online. Key needs: fast menu browsing, simple checkout, flexible payment (online or cash on delivery). Pain points: slow or complicated ordering experiences, limited payment options.

### Secondary: Admins / Ops Team

Restaurant staff who view and manage incoming orders. Key needs: real-time visibility of orders, clear order status management. Pain points: manual processes that slow down fulfilment.

## Product Boundaries

### In Scope

- Browse menu and place orders online
- Online payment (card/wallet) and cash on delivery
- Real-time order tracking for customers
- Admin dashboard for staff to view and manage orders

### Out of Scope

- Third-party delivery driver management
- Multi-restaurant marketplace
- Table reservations or dine-in booking

## Strategic Direction

Current focus is growing the customer base — acquiring new customers and building retention through a smooth, reliable ordering experience. Near-term priorities include a polished end-to-end ordering flow and maximising conversion from menu browse to completed order.
