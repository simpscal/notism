# Discovery Session Pattern

Before creating or modifying any tracker artifacts, reach shared clarity with the PO.

## Steps

1. **Synthesise first.** Write out your current understanding:
   - Who is the user?
   - What do they want to achieve?
   - What does "done" look like?
   - What constraints or boundaries apply?

2. **Surface every gap.** Identify all ambiguities, conflicting signals, and missing context that would materially change scope.

3. **Open the dialogue.** Use `AskUserQuestion` to present understanding and ask all blocking questions in one structured message:
   - Lead: *"Here is what I understand — please correct anything wrong."*
   - Follow: *"Before I proceed, I need to clarify:"* — list specific questions.
   - Do NOT drip-feed questions one at a time.

4. **Incorporate and iterate.** After each PO response, re-synthesise. Repeat until fully unambiguous.

5. **Confirm alignment.** State final understanding and confirm with PO before proceeding.
